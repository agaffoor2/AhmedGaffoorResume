LBMA Custom Information System Project

Project Description

The LBMA Custom Information System Project was an academic assignment simulating a real-world IT planning effort for the Lake Baldwin Music Academy (LBMA). The academy's existing reliance on a slow, paper-based system for student registration and scheduling caused delays, errors, and administrative burden. As the team Knights Tech Solutions, our objective was to propose a specialized digital system to streamline these processes, reduce mistakes, and make operations more orderly. The project's success was rigorously defined by its Measurable Organizational Value (MOV), which required reducing student processing time from three days to under 30 minutes, increasing enrollment capacity by 15%, and cutting scheduling errors by 90% within six months of system deployment. Our deliverables included a feasibility analysis comparing alternatives and a comprehensive IT Quality Management Plan detailing quality philosophy, metrics, and verification and validation activities.
